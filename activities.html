Recent Activities

Our ongoing efforts to support Ukrainian communities

Food Distribution in Kharkiv

May 5, 2023

Distributed 1,000 food packages to families in recently liberated areas around Kharkiv. The operation reached over 3,500 people including elderly citizens and children.

Demining Operation in Sumy Region

April 18, 2023

Successfully cleared 15 hectares of farmland, allowing local farmers to safely resume agricultural activities. This operation helped restore economic activity in the region.

Medical Supply Delivery to Mykolaiv

March 30, 2023

Delivered essential medical supplies worth $50,000 to three hospitals in the Mykolaiv region, including emergency equipment, medications, and surgical tools.

Education Support in Kyiv

March 15, 2023

Provided laptops and internet connectivity to 200 students affected by the conflict, enabling them to continue their education remotely.

Winter Relief Program in Donetsk Region

February 20, 2023

Distributed winter clothing, blankets, and heating equipment to 500 families living in areas with damaged infrastructure and limited heating options.

Community Center Opening in Zaporizhzhia

January 25, 2023

Inaugurated a new community center providing psychological support, job training, and children's activities for displaced families in the Zaporizhzhia region.
