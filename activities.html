Our Recent Activities

Explore our recent humanitarian activities across Ukraine. These efforts are part of our ongoing commitment to support communities affected by the war.

Food Distribution in Kharkiv

May 5, 2023 Kharkiv Region, Ukraine

Distributed 1,000 food packages to families in recently liberated areas around Kharkiv, providing essential nutrition for approximately 3,500 individuals.

Demining Operation in Sumy Region

April 18, 2023 Sumy Region, Ukraine

Successfully cleared 15 hectares of farmland, allowing local farmers to safely resume agricultural activities and secure food production for the region.

Medical Supply Delivery to Mykolaiv

March 30, 2023 Mykolaiv Region, Ukraine

Delivered essential medical supplies worth $50,000 to three hospitals in the Mykolaiv region, including trauma kits, surgical equipment, and medications.

Community Kitchen Support in Zaporizhzhia

March 15, 2023 Zaporizhzhia, Ukraine

Provided equipment and supplies to a community kitchen serving 500 hot meals daily to internally displaced persons and vulnerable residents.

Mobile Medical Clinic in Donetsk Region

February 28, 2023 Donetsk Region, Ukraine

Operated a mobile medical clinic that provided basic healthcare services to 12 villages with limited access to medical facilities.

Mine Risk Education for Schools

February 10, 2023 Kherson Region, Ukraine

Conducted mine risk education sessions for 1,500 schoolchildren and 200 teachers in recently de-occupied areas of Kherson region.
