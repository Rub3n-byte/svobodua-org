Who We Are

Ukraine Aid Network is a dedicated humanitarian organization committed to supporting Ukrainian communities affected by the ongoing conflict through direct aid, demining operations, and rebuilding assistance.

Our Story

Founded with a mission to make a difference

Ukraine Aid Network was founded in March 2022, shortly after the full-scale invasion of Ukraine began. What started as a grassroots effort by a small group of concerned citizens quickly evolved into an organized humanitarian network with hundreds of volunteers and partners across Ukraine and internationally.


Our founders, having witnessed the devastating impact of the war on civilian populations, were determined to create a responsive and effective organization that could deliver critical assistance to those most in need. From the distribution of emergency food supplies to coordinating with demining experts, our organization has expanded its scope to address the most pressing challenges facing Ukrainian communities.


Today, Ukraine Aid Network operates throughout the country, with a particular focus on frontline communities and recently liberated territories. Our team works tirelessly to reach those who might otherwise be forgotten, ensuring that aid reaches every corner of Ukraine affected by the conflict.

Volunteer With Us

Join our team and make a direct impact

Ukraine Aid Network relies on the dedication and skills of volunteers from across Ukraine and around the world. Whether you're based locally or internationally, there are many ways you can contribute to our mission:


  • On-the-ground support: Help with food distribution, logistics, and community outreach in Ukraine.
  • Remote assistance: Provide translation services, social media management, grant writing, and administrative support from anywhere in the world.
  • Technical expertise: Share your professional skills in areas such as medicine, engineering, agriculture, or technology.
  • Fundraising: Organize events or campaigns to help raise funds and awareness for our programs.

Your time and talents can make a real difference in the lives of Ukrainians affected by the conflict. Join us in our mission to provide aid, restore safety, and rebuild communities.
