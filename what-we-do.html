What We Do

Ukraine Aid Network implements targeted programs to address the most pressing needs of Ukrainian communities affected by the conflict, from food security to demining operations.

Food Distribution Program

Ensuring food security for vulnerable populations

Our Food Distribution Program delivers essential food supplies to communities across Ukraine, with a focus on:


  • Emergency Food Packages: Providing ready-to-eat meals and non-perishable food items to families in active conflict zones.
  • Community Kitchens: Supporting local community kitchens that prepare hot meals for vulnerable residents.
  • Agricultural Support: Helping farmers in affected areas resume food production through seeds, tools, and technical assistance.
  • Nutrition Programs: Ensuring children, pregnant women, and elderly individuals receive proper nutrition through specialized food packages.

Since the beginning of our operations, we have distributed over 500,000 meals across 150 communities in Ukraine, reaching more than 50,000 individuals in need.

Demining Initiative

Making Ukrainian lands safe again

One of our most critical programs is our Demining Initiative, which works to address the dangerous legacy of unexploded ordnance and mines across Ukraine:


  • Professional Demining Teams: We partner with certified demining experts to safely clear mines and unexploded ordnance.
  • Agricultural Land Clearance: Prioritizing farmland to enable food production and economic recovery.
  • Residential Area Safety: Clearing homes and community spaces to allow for safe return of displaced residents.
  • Mine Risk Education: Conducting awareness programs to teach communities about the dangers of unexploded ordinance.

To date, our teams have safely cleared over 120 areas across eastern and southern Ukraine, making these lands safe for agriculture, housing, and community activities.

Medical Assistance Program

Supporting healthcare services for affected communities

Our Medical Assistance Program works to strengthen healthcare services in conflict-affected regions through:


  • Medical Supply Delivery: Providing medications, equipment, and supplies to hospitals and clinics in need.
  • Mobile Health Units: Deploying mobile teams to reach communities with limited access to healthcare.
  • Emergency Medical Training: Conducting first aid and trauma response training for community members.
  • Psychological Support: Offering mental health services to address the psychological impact of conflict.

We have delivered over 15,000 medical supply packages to healthcare facilities across Ukraine and provided direct medical assistance to thousands of individuals through our mobile health units.

Education Support Program

Ensuring learning continues despite the challenges

Our Education Support Program works to ensure that Ukrainian children and youth continue to have access to quality education despite the disruptions caused by the conflict:


  • School Rehabilitation: Repairing and rebuilding damaged educational facilities.
  • Learning Materials: Distributing books, supplies, and educational resources to students.
  • Digital Learning Support: Providing computers, internet access, and technical support for remote education.
  • Teacher Training: Supporting educators with resources and training for crisis education.

Through these efforts, we have helped over 200 schools continue operations and supported more than 30,000 students in maintaining their educational journey during these challenging times.
