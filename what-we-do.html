What We Do

Our programs and initiatives are designed to provide immediate relief and long-term support to Ukrainian communities affected by the conflict.

Food Distribution

Providing essential nutrition to those in need

Our food distribution program reaches thousands of Ukrainians in frontline communities and displaced persons across the country. We provide:

  • Emergency food packages with non-perishable items
  • Fresh produce from local farmers when possible
  • Special nutrition packages for vulnerable groups including children and elderly
  • Community kitchen support in collective centers

Working with local partners and volunteers, we ensure that food reaches even the most remote or recently liberated areas where commercial supply chains have been disrupted.

Demining Initiative

Making Ukrainian lands safe again

Ukraine is now one of the most heavily mined countries in the world. Our demining initiative works to restore safety to Ukrainian communities by:

  • Partnering with professional demining organizations
  • Focusing on agricultural lands to restore food production
  • Clearing residential areas to allow safe return of displaced people
  • Conducting mine risk education in affected communities

Every hectare cleared represents lives saved and livelihoods restored. Our program prioritizes areas based on humanitarian impact and works closely with local authorities to coordinate efforts.

Medical Assistance

Supporting healthcare services across Ukraine

The conflict has placed enormous strain on Ukraine's healthcare system. Our medical assistance program helps by:

  • Delivering essential medical supplies to hospitals and clinics
  • Providing specialized equipment for trauma care
  • Supporting mobile medical units reaching isolated communities
  • Facilitating telemedicine services where physical access is limited

We work directly with healthcare professionals to understand specific needs and deliver targeted assistance that makes a real difference in patient outcomes.
